About PrintFix Technologies

Kenya's trusted enterprise printer solutions partner — reliable, certified, and built for organizational scale.

Our Story

Built to Serve Enterprise Kenya

PrintFix Technologies was founded to solve a critical gap in Kenya's market: enterprise organizations — government ministries, corporates, NGOs — were being served by small, informal repair shops unable to meet the reliability, accountability, and speed that institutional clients require.

We built PrintFix from the ground up as an enterprise-grade service organization: certified technicians, SLA-backed contracts, genuine OEM parts, and a management team experienced in serving large institutions across East Africa.

Today, PrintFix manages print infrastructure for government ministries, leading banks, multinational NGOs, and corporate organizations across Kenya — delivering uptime, cost control, and the accountability that institutional clients demand.

Strategic Partnership

Aligned with MFI Group

PrintFix Technologies operates in strategic alignment with MFI Group — East Africa's leading technology solutions provider. This partnership gives PrintFix clients access to the same enterprise-grade standards, authorized partnerships, and institutional client networks that MFI has built over four decades across 18 African countries.

MFI partners with Kyocera, Ricoh, Canon, and HP Enterprise — brands that PrintFix is authorized to service. Organizations that are MFI clients benefit from seamlessly integrated printer support through PrintFix.
